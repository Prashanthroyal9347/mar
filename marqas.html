About Einstein

Albert Einstein was a theoretical physicist known for the theory of relativity and the famous equation E = mc². He believed in the power of simplicity, which also applies to writing clean and effective HTML.

HTML Tags

HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the foundation of web pages. It includes elements like:

  • <h1> to <h6> - Headings
  • <p> - Paragraphs
  • <a> - Links
  • <img> - Images

Bookmarking in HTML

Bookmarking helps users jump to specific sections of a web page using the id attribute and <a href="#id">.
